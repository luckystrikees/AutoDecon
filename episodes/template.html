The Physics of Social Fatigue

Autistic burnout is often described as a psychological issue. This episode explores why social exhaustion may instead reflect energetic limits within the nervous system. We examine the biological cost of sustained social adaptation, and why autistic cognition may operate under different energetic constraints than typical social models assume.
