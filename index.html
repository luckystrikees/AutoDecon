About the Podcast

Autism Deconstructed explores autism through biology, energy, and lived experience. Each episode examines how autistic cognition interacts with the environments we live in — from everyday social situations to broader social expectations. The goal is simple: to make complex ideas about autism clear, accessible, and grounded in real experience.
